⋅˚₊‧ Taylor Swift ‧₊˚ ⋅

Folklore

Folklore (2020) is Taylor Swift's indie folk-inspired album, featuring introspective lyrics and a more subdued, atmospheric sound. It explores themes of love, loss, and nostalgia, with standout tracks like "cardigan" and "exile." The album was critically acclaimed for its storytelling and emotional depth.

1989

1989 (2014) is Taylor Swift's fifth studio album, marking her full transition from country to pop. It features upbeat, 80s-inspired synth-pop soundscapes and explores themes of love, heartbreak, and self-discovery. Hits like "Shake It Off," "Blank Space," and "Style" made it a commercial success, earning widespread acclaim and solidifying Swift as a pop powerhouse.

Evermore

Evermore (2020) is Taylor Swift's folk-inspired follow-up to Folklore, featuring introspective lyrics and a wintry, atmospheric sound. The album explores themes of love and loss, with standout tracks like "willow" and "champagne problems." It was well-received for its emotional depth and storytelling.

Lover

Lover (2019) is Taylor Swift's pop album centered around love, self-acceptance, and nostalgia. With tracks like "ME!," "You Need to Calm Down," and "Lover," it features a lighter, more colorful sound and was praised for its catchy, heartfelt lyrics.

Diary 001 (2021) is Clairo's debut EP under her real name, Shelley. It blends R&B, indie, and electronic sounds, with intimate lyrics exploring love and self-reflection. The EP showcases a raw, experimental side of Clairo's music.
